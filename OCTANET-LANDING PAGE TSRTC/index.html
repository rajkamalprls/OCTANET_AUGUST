Telangana State Road Transport Corporation

Safe, Reliable, and Affordable Bus Travel To The People

01

Garuda+

Garuda Bus is a premium intercity bus service in Telangana known for its comfortable seating, air-conditioning, modern amenities, and emphasis on passenger safety. It provides inter-city connectivity, reserved seating, and the convenience of online booking. The service varies slightly across different state transport corporations, with each offering its unique features and facilities.

02

Rajadhani

Rajadhani Bus is a premium intercity bus service operated by TSRTC. These air-conditioned buses offer modern amenities, comfortable seating, and a strong emphasis on passenger safety. They provide inter-city connectivity, reserved seating, and the convenience of online booking.

03

Super Luxury

Super Luxury Buses are operated by TSRTC, providing a higher level of comfort and convenience to passengers for their intercity travel needs.It offers luxurious and spacious seating, onboard entertainment.

TSRTC

TSRTC is an essential part of Telangana's transportation infrastructure, providing a lifeline for commuters and contributing to the socio-economic development of the state. It continues to evolve and adapt to the changing needs of the population, making public transportation more efficient and passenger-friendly.

It operates an extensive network of buses, providing inter-city and inter-state transportation. TSRTC focuses on passenger safety, offers online booking, and contributes to social welfare.
